chronicle prompt: add a third A/B variant `chronicle-descriptive.md` and make it the active default. It is the loosened prompt with two changes: places and people are named by what they are and where they stand (the drowned vault beneath the causeway, the marsh-wardens) rather than by minting proper nouns — a proper name is used only where the record supplies one — and it carries more varied few-shot examples (a failed delve, a won negotiation, a sacrifice after a botched evasion) to keep the model off a single prototype.
