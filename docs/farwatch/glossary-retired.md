# Farwatch — Retired vocabulary

Terms once used in Farwatch that have since been retired or renamed. Kept here as the single record of
_why_, so the reasons aren't relitigated and the old words aren't reused by accident. The living
vocabulary is [glossary.md](glossary.md); this doc is its graveyard.

Each entry is `**Old term** → **new term** — why`.

---

**Covenant** → **Compact** — _Retired for IP distance._ "Covenant" is the signature term in _Ars
Magica_ for the same conceptual object: a community of practitioners and the home they hold. Too
direct a borrow to ship. **Compact** replaces it — a binding pact among free wills (fitting, since a
seeker can break it), and one that opens an in-world etymology the living glossary can lean on: the
eroded name of a mythic-age agreement among the elevated beings who became the **patrons**, its
original meaning long since lost. See **Compact** in [glossary.md](glossary.md).
