Farwatch — UI mockups

Two structural directions for the same Desk + Missive content. Reading-first is the current direction; ui-heavy is the earlier dashboard-in-costume, kept for comparison.

Document grammar (reading)

  • Document / warm-melancholy — reading
    a candle in a cold room, not a hearth — worn-ledger paper, faded ink; readability pass (wider paper, ~22px, ink bloom + discretionary ligatures)
  • Document / warm paper on the cabinet field
    the warm-melancholy reader's paper + ink, unchanged, set on the hybrid's cool smoke-and-stone surround — slow drifting smoke; the warm page against the cold room

Collection compare-table grammar (deciding)

  • ★ dynamic — switchable paper
    the working mockup going forward — smoky-viewport table with chrome dropdowns for paper style (worn-bright / ledger / card / austere / clean) and colour (paper × ink pairings), swapping every paper surface at once; driven by paper.css + paper-color.css
  • hybrid — cool cabinet + warm documents
    the leading direction — warm parchment dossiers pulled into the lane; drifting smoke field over the whole page; handwritten roster checklist — the people warm against the system's cold
  • hybrid — grey page · smoke window
    background treatment: a flat grey page with the smoke confined to a window cut into it; the going-tray sits out on the grey
  • hybrid — warm page · smoke window
    same window, but the surround is the warm-melancholy brown candle-pool instead of grey — a warm room around a cool smoke window
  • hybrid — smoke page · flat slab
    the flip: smoke drifts across the whole page; the documents rest on a flat grey slab raised above it
  • warm / romantic — lighter, larger
    candlelit lit parchment on a lighter walnut field; type bumped (root 19px)
  • warm-melancholy — worn ledger
    a candle in a cold room — worn paper, faded ink, gold dialed down, flat found-and-used controls (no gloss/gilt)
  • austere / monastic — Spectral (grey reference)
    kept as a simpler cool-grey reference point — bone panels, hairline rules, small-caps, larger type

Party-building: anchor · roster rail · dossiers laid side by side (touch a name) · forming-party tray. Same grammar as Board-review.

Studies & tools

  • ★ document designer — author × occasion × paper × ink, weathered by condition
    the composer: a tab per entity (Paper / Ink / Author / Occasion / Condition), each with save/load presets (localStorage), driven by document-model.js — bleed is derived (absorbency × flow) and age weathers paper + ink in script, not CSS
  • ★ paper / ink colour swatches (dynamic)
    every curated paper × ink colour combination, rendered on selectable paper style + ink style — the live tool for choosing colours; driven by paper.css + paper-color.css + ink.css
  • ★ ink exploration (dynamic)
    sweep the writing — font family + weight dropdowns, a range of font sizes, an ink-cycle hand on the reading sample — on a selectable paper style + colour
  • ★ ink-cycle designer (dynamic)
    the reading view at 1.35rem with every dial — paper · colour · face · weight · bleed — plus full ink-cycle tuning (granularity, dip length, floor, weight range, darkness, blobs, walk) to design the reservoir model against a real passage
  • paper / palette swatches (static, original)
    the earlier hand-built swatch sheet (six warm tones + a cool wildcard) — superseded by the dynamic one
